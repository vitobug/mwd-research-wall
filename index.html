Research Wall

How prepared are customers for the changing future of CRM?
About this research
Learning Goals
  • How prepared do customers feel for the changing future of CRM?
  • How can Salesforce equip them for what's next — product functionality, data ecosystem, and beyond?
  • Source participants for ongoing research throughout the year
Guiding Principles
  • Reciprocity — Participants' voices will shape our product development
  • Learning — We learn from customers; they learn from each other
  • Inspiration — Participants are energized to imagine the future of their business with Salesforce
Method

The wall had two sections. The first was a readiness meter — a gradient spectrum bar anchored from "Slow down, I feel left behind" to "Bring it on, I feel ready." Each attendee received one star sticker to place anywhere on the bar to represent how prepared they felt for the Agentic Enterprise.

The second section posed three open-ended questions about AI readiness. Attendees wrote responses on sticky notes and posted them under each question. They could also add star stickers to any sticky notes they agreed with, acting as an upvote.
